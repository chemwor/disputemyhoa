Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

When you use our services at disputemyhoa.com, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

Document Information
  • HOA notices and documents you upload for review

Educational Service Disclaimer

Important: DisputeMyHOA provides educational information and drafting assistance only. We do not provide legal advice or legal representation. All content is for informational purposes to help you understand your HOA documents and draft professional responses.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Regular security audits and updates
